Notation: where x be I: Introductory, D: Intermediate, A: Advanced, or empty.

I – The instructor will introduce the topic at the level required. It is not necessary for the student to have seen the material before.

D – There may be a reminder or review, but the student is expected to have seen and been tested on the material before taking the course.

A – It is expected that the student can apply the knowledge without prompting (e.g. no review).
